FAQs

Got questions? We got answers

MindTalk is our monthly event that aims to empower students, in areas beyond academics. We concentrate on developing life skills like emotional intelligence, resilience, effective communication and leadership in addition, to technical knowledge. Our aim is to support students in excelling across facets of their lives.

Our MindTalk event is accessible to high school, university and graduate students. We also welcome educators, professionals, and anyone interested in personal and professional growth.

You can attend our upcoming events physically at Sqi College of ICT or virtually through the ‘Events’ section of our website. Simply click on the “Watch Live” button, the 2nd Monday of every month at 10:00 AM, and you’re all set!

MindTalk events are free to attend at no cost.

You can stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter through the form at the bottom section of our website. You’ll receive regular updates on upcoming events, speaker announcements, and more.

We are constantly seeking to team up with individuals and groups. If you are keen, on becoming a speaker or partner kindly head over to our “Contact” section below. Get in touch, with us through the email provided. We are excited to see how we can collaborate together.
